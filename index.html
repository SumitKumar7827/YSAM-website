Our Mission

Building
Future Champs

The Yogasana Sports Association Mauritius (YSAM) is the official governing body dedicated to promoting Yogasana as a competitive sport nationwide. We provide structured training, international-standard competitions, and holistic athlete development programs.

To establish Mauritius as a leading nation in competitive Yogasana by 2030, with athletes competing at World Championships and the Asian Games. We aim to develop 100+ international-level athletes and host regional championships.

We offer specialized training programs for all age groups, led by certified international coaches with expertise in sports yoga and competitive asanas. Programs include beginner foundations, competitive training, and elite athlete development.

YSAM is recognized by the World Yogasana Federation and Asian Yogasana Sports Association. Our athletes compete in international championships and we follow global standards for judging and competition rules.
